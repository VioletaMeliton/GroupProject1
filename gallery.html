Our Services

I offer a free first training for all new clients who are interesterd in the program. I usually make parents give me a recording of their training or games, so I can asses their level of mastery whether it is in the group or individually. In the consultation I assess individual goals and assist in setting benchmarks.

Camps

Every season I commence camps for group sessions of 15-20 students. The trainings are comprehensive that include a warmup, pool training, and step-by-step baskebtall learning. Players learn critical individual skills that enhances their ability as a team player. They are also coached on handling the mental and emotional aspects of basketball.
